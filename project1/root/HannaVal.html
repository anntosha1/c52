About Me

Happy to see you on my page! I'm Hanna Val and I'm an immigrant from Ukraine who is looking for new opportunities in life and want to make work that will make me and people for whom I'll work happy. I believe that technologies make our life easier and better (educational web-sites, medical programming, studying programming). I want to be a software engineer and plan to be good at the next software developing technologies: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, C#, Python, React, SASS/LESS, Git'/Github, npm. I see myself in Front End Development because I am a "visual" person. I like to see the result of my work with my own eyes. I feel interest and desire to solve the problem. I'm not afraid to learn some new technologies, because it impresses me how they work and makes development easier and faster (Flex, Grid, BEM, Libraries, frameworks). My goal is to become professional in Front End Development and making quality and beautiful sites and applications.
